Professional & Surgeon Partnerships

Clinical Collaboration & Client Referrals

As an independent Registered Nurse operating in the high-acuity concierge space, Tricia Connolly, RN serves as a trusted clinical partner for premier surgical teams, concierge physicians, care coordinators, and family offices throughout Beverly Hills and Greater Los Angeles.

Operating under the strict instruction of the patient's attending physician, Tricia translates complex post-discharge plans into meticulous home-care schedules, bridging the gap between clinical discharge and long-term home wellness.

Collaboration Model

  • attending Physician Compliance: Direct adherence to your specific post-operative care plans, medication orders, and activity limitations.
  • Comprehensive Clinical Logging: Monitoring and recording vital signs, wound healing, drain outputs, and intake/output metrics with daily status updates sent directly to your practice.
  • Absolute Discretion & NDAs: All care is delivered under strict privacy protocols, safeguarding the identity and records of high-profile, executive, and celebrity clientele.
  • Luxury Hotel Care: Safe and professional clinical nursing coordination in private residences, luxury estates, and all premier luxury hotels and resorts in Beverly Hills and Greater Los Angeles.

Luxury Hotel & Resort Care Coordination
