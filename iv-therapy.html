Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide custom IV formulations at home?

Yes, Tricia administers doctor-prescribed IV hydration, vitamins, and specialized clinical infusions in the comfort of your private residence.

What is the difference between palliative care and hospice?

Palliative care focuses on symptom relief and quality of life at any stage of a serious illness, often alongside curative treatment. Hospice is specifically dedicated to compassionate end-of-life comfort. Tricia provides expert support for both.

How do you ensure clinical safety during in-home IV therapy?

As an experienced Registered Nurse, Tricia performs full baseline vital checks, monitors infusion rates meticulously, and maintains an emergency kit for any adverse reactions.
