Frequently Asked Questions

How soon should I book post-operative care?

It is highly recommended to coordinate care at least 2-4 weeks before your scheduled procedure. This allows Tricia to consult with your surgeon, obtain pre-op orders, and prepare the recovery environment in advance.

Do you coordinate directly with my surgeon?

Yes. Direct clinical communication is vital. Tricia interfaces with your surgical team to obtain post-op orders, medication guidelines, and follows up on your recovery metrics.

What types of surgeries do you specialize in recovering?

She specializes in plastic/cosmetic surgery recovery, complex reconstructive procedures, and general major surgeries requiring meticulous clinical oversight.

What geographic areas do you cover?

Primary service areas include Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Santa Monica, Brentwood, Malibu, and the wider West Los Angeles area.
